### Description: 

Berry notes, light roast with a floral aroma.

### The Story:

Deep within the lush highlands of the Sidamo region, where coffee is said to have been born, grows this exceptional heirloom variety. Ethiopia Sidamo is a testament to the harmony between tradition and nature. Cultivated by smallholder farmers at high altitudes, the coffee cherries mature slowly under the shade of native trees, allowing the beans to develop a complexity rarely found elsewhere.

When brewed, this light roast unveils a delicate floral bouquet reminiscent of jasmine, followed by a vibrant, fruit-forward acidity. Each cup tells the story of the ancient forests of Ethiopia, offering a bright and sophisticated experience that lingers on the palate.
