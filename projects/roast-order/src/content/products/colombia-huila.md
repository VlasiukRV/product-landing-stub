### Description:

Balanced body, notes of caramel, cocoa, and a hint of citrus.

### The Story:

The Huila region is often considered the heart of Colombian coffee production, thanks to its unique volcanic soil and ideal microclimate. Our Colombia Huila is a medium roast that strikes a perfect balance between comfort and character. It is the kind of coffee that feels like home—warm, reliable, and deeply satisfying.

As you take your first sip, you’ll notice the rich, velvety sweetness of caramelized sugar and dark chocolate. This foundation is beautifully punctuated by a subtle brightness of orange zest, creating a cup that is both approachable for daily drinking and nuanced enough for a slow, mindful morning ritual.
