📍 Remote | 🕒 Full-time | 🌍 International Team

### About the Role
**UPTARGET is looking for a Full-Stack Developer with a strong backend focus** to help us build and modernize core systems across our retail, logistics, ERP, and eCommerce environments.

You’ll be joining a cross-functional team led by a Full-Stack Team Lead and play a key role in bringing more ownership, speed, and execution capability in-house. You’ll work on a wide variety of systems—including internal tools, backend services, APIs, ERP/POS integrations, and customer-facing applications—with a strong emphasis on backend architecture, business logic, and integrations.

This is a high-ownership, hands-on development role that supports our shift away from fragmented outsourcing and toward a focused, fast-moving internal engineering team.



### Key Responsibilities
* Develop and maintain backend services, APIs, integrations, and internal systems using .NET, PHP, and/or Node.js.
* Contribute to frontend interfaces in React when needed (minimal).
* Participate in code reviews, testing, deployment, and production support.
* Work across domains: eCommerce, ERP, logistics, internal tools, customer-facing apps.
* Collaborate with team leads, DevOps, and product managers to scope and deliver features quickly and reliably.
* Help identify refactoring or rebuild opportunities across legacy services.
* Continuously improve team velocity and code quality.

### Qualifications
* 5+ years of experience in full-stack or backend development roles.
* Proficiency in at least one backend language/framework: .NET, PHP, Node.js.
* Familiarity with SQL or Oracle DBs; exposure to MongoDB or PostgreSQL is a plus.
* Comfortable working with APIs, business logic, integrations, and real-world constraints.
* Some experience with React or willingness to contribute to frontend work when needed.
* Strong sense of ownership and the ability to deliver independently and as part of a team.

### Bonus Points
* Experience in retail, logistics, or ERP-integrated systems.
* Familiarity with CI/CD pipelines, Docker, or DevOps collaboration.
* Exposure to performance optimization, legacy code refactoring, or system modernization projects.
* Ability to work in a fast-paced, delivery-focused environment and thrive under evolving requirements.
