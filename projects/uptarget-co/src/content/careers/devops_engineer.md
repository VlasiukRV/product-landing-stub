📍 Remote | 🕒 Full-time | 🌍 International Team

### About the Role:
**UPTARGET** is looking for a **DevOps Engineer** to work on building and maintaining the infrastructure behind a modern, data-driven e-commerce platform. You will help ensure our systems are scalable, reliable, and optimized for performance, enabling seamless delivery of new features and supporting the growth of our brand ecosystem.

### What you’ll be doing:
- Develop and maintain CI/CD pipelines  
- Design, implement, and maintain cloud infrastructure  
- Minimize reliability and security-related risks  
- Perform Infrastructure performance analysis and optimization  
- Design and build tools for monitoring, testing, and diagnosis of systems  
- Actively support and work closely with the development team
### Requirements:
- 5+ years of solid and proven experience in DevOps for a Cloud or SaaS (AWS & GCP) -must  
- Experience with High Availability, Scalability, and Reliability solutions  
- Experience with networking  
- Experience developing CI/CD pipelines  
- Proficient in scripting — Bash/Python  
- Experience with managing IaC stacks (development & implementation)  
- Experience with Kubernetes  
- Excellent team player  
- Great communication and collaboration skills  
- Strong problem-solving skills and ability to work under pressure  
- Experience working remotely — availability to work on-call shifts  
- Proficiency in English

### Nice to Have:
- Experience in Multi-Cloud solutions  
- Good understanding of Application Lifecycle Management  
- Experience with CDNs  
- Experience with Databases (MongoDB, Postgres)
