### **About the Role**
**UPTARGET is seeking an experienced and visionary Chief Technology Officer (CTO)** to lead the technical strategy and architecture for a group of digital products — a short-video platform, a digital wallet, and a creative marketplace. This is a hands-on leadership role with both strategic and execution responsibilities. You'll make end-to-end technology decisions, ensuring all systems are scalable, secure, and integrated.
### **Key Responsibilities:**
* Own and define the overall technical strategy and roadmap.
* Architect and oversee the development of scalable, secure, and reliable systems.
* Lead and mentor a high-performing engineering team.
* Collaborate closely with product managers, designers, and business stakeholders to align on vision and execution. 
* Ensure high standards of quality, performance, and security across the entire technology stack. 
* Contribute to mobile development planning and integration (basic technical understanding required).

### **Requirements:**
* Proven experience as a **CTO, Head of Engineering,** or **Tech Lead.**
* 7+ years of hands-on experience in software development and architecture using **Node.js** and **React**.
* Demonstrated success in leading engineering teams and building scalable products. 
* Strong expertise in software architecture, system design, and engineering best practices. 
* In-depth knowledge of cloud infrastructure (AWS, GCP, or equivalent). 
* Basic understanding of mobile development principles and integration. 
* Excellent leadership, communication, and cross-functional collaboration skills. 
* Experience working in fast-paced environments and making strategic technical decisions. 
* Passion for innovation, technology, and making an impact. 
* Upper-Intermediate English or higher.
